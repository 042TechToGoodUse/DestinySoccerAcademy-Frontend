About Us

Our organisation is a young football acaled called DESTINY SOCCER ACADEMY (DSA) based in Enugu and established to develop grass root football in Nigeria, Established in 2016, DSA has the vision to harness young football talents who will become world-class footballers, model citizens, and world leaders. This they hope to do through innovative football development programs and activities as well as educational, health, and citizenship programs meant to empower young people and strengthen families.
